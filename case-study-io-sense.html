iO Sense Smart Charging Base

Reimagining smart oral care by integrating AI-powered coaching directly into the charging base, eliminating friction and delivering real-time guidance for the best possible oral health outcomes.

Role
Design Lead
Timeline
2020 - 2022
Team
Design, Engineering, Marketing
Category
Product Design, UX, IoT

The Challenge

Despite developing an award-winning Oral-B mobile app with sophisticated AI-powered brushing guidance, we identified a critical friction point: consumers weren't using it consistently. The reality of bringing your phone into the bathroom twice daily, opening an app, and keeping it active during the entire brushing routine created unnecessary barriers to getting the coaching they needed.

Our vision was clear: create a Smart Oral Care Products portfolio that would deliver coaching, guidance, and feedback seamlessly—helping consumers know they're getting the best results for their time, money, and effort invested in their oral health.

The Core Question

How simple could we make an interface that ensured consumers got the best experience with the least friction on a twice-daily basis?

My Role & Approach

As Design Lead on a three-person core team (alongside an Engineer and a Marketer), I brought both my vision and the full capabilities of my Industrial and UX/UI Design teams to bear on this challenge. My ownership spanned the critical early stages:

  • Early Product Concept Creation: Defining the strategic direction and exploring opportunity spaces
  • Form Development: Translating abstract concepts into tangible product forms
  • Prototyping: Building functional prototypes to validate technical feasibility and user experience
  • Visualization: Creating assets for social media-based behavioral research to test concepts with real users

The Design Process

1

Exploration Phase

We began by exploring three distinct configurations for a smart charging base, each with different approaches to delivering guidance and feedback. This exploration phase was critical in understanding the solution space and technical constraints.

2

Oral-B Guide Learning

We even launched an intermediate product—Oral-B Guide—that integrated Amazon Alexa directly into the charging base. This bold experiment taught us valuable lessons about complexity vs. simplicity, and what features truly served the core use case versus what created additional friction.

3

Simplification & Focus

Drawing from our learnings, we returned to first principles: what's the simplest possible interface that delivers maximum value? This led us to the iO Sense concept—coaching integrated directly into the charging base with an elegant LED guidance system and digital display.

4

Strategic Rationale

The charging base emerged as the optimal solution for four key reasons: (1) easily viewable while brushing, (2) more cost-effective than a smart mirror, (3) constantly powered to support the interface, and (4) already part of the user's daily routine. It was hiding in plain sight.

The Innovation

iO Sense represents a category first: the first time any power toothbrush brand had built an AI-based position detection and brushing coach system into any interface—much less one not on the brush itself. This breakthrough eliminated the need for phone interaction entirely, providing real-time coaching on time, pressure, and coverage directly via the charger.

Key Features

  • 6-Zone LED Guidance System: Visual representation of mouth zones with real-time coverage feedback
  • Built-in Digital Clock & Timer: Dual-purpose display showing time and 2-minute brushing countdown
  • Pressure Sensor Feedback: Direct visual indication when brushing too hard, too soft, or just right
  • AI Position Detection: Tracks brushing across all 3 tooth surfaces without requiring phone
  • 3-Hour Fast Magnetic Charging: Secure magnetic connection with rapid recharge capability
  • App Sync Capability: Optional deeper insights for users who want detailed tracking

Key Learnings

This project reinforced several principles that guide my approach to design leadership:

Simplicity Through Iteration

Sometimes the path to simplicity requires exploring complexity first. Oral-B Guide taught us what features truly mattered versus what created friction.

Design for Daily Reality

The best technology disappears into routine. By integrating coaching into an object already in the user's daily workflow, we removed barriers to adoption.

Strategic Constraints Drive Innovation

Choosing the charging base over a handle display or smart mirror wasn't just cost-driven—it forced us to innovate within meaningful constraints, leading to a more elegant solution.

Outcome

The iO Sense Smart Charging Base achieved what we set out to do: eliminate friction in smart oral care while delivering coaching that genuinely improves consumer outcomes. By generating $100M+ in first-year revenue targets, achieving 30% conversion of manual brush users in key markets, and establishing a new category benchmark, we proved that thoughtful design leadership combined with strategic execution creates lasting business value.

More importantly, we created a product that consumers love—evidenced by 4.5/5 stars across 1,000+ reviews and recognition from both industry awards and dental professionals worldwide. This wasn't just about winning awards or hitting revenue targets; it was about fundamentally improving how millions of people approach their oral health every day.
