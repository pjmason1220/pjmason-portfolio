iO Sense Connected Charging Base

Revolutionary smart charging stand that serves as a brushing coach, providing real-time feedback without requiring the app to be open. Integrated LED guidance system and digital display transform the charging base from passive accessory to active coaching tool, making personalized dental care effortless.

  • Real-time coverage guidance via LED light array
  • Built-in digital clock and 2-minute timer
  • Pressure sensor feedback directly on charger
  • 6-zone mouth mapping visualization
  • 3-hour fast magnetic charging
Innovation:

First-in-category integration of coaching technology into charging base, eliminating need for phone interaction during brushing routine
